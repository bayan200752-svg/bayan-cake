🍓 Strawberry Cake

A fresh and fruity delight

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, mashed
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Fresh strawberries for decoration

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and flour a 9-inch cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Add mashed strawberries and vanilla extract, mix well.
  5. In another bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt.
  6. Add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients alternately with milk.
  7. Mix until just combined - don't overmix.
  8. Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for 35-40 minutes.
  9. Let cool completely before decorating with fresh strawberries.
  10. Serve and enjoy your strawberry cake!
