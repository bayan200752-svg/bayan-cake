🍫 Chocolate Cake

A rich and moist chocolate cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and flour a 9-inch cake pan.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Beat with an electric mixer for 2 minutes until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake for 30-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Remove from oven and let cool in the pan for 10 minutes.
  7. Transfer to a wire rack and cool completely before frosting.
  8. Enjoy your delicious chocolate cake!
