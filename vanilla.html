🧁 Vanilla Cake

A classic and timeless flavor

Ingredients

  • 2½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1¾ cups sugar
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Buttercream frosting (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy (about 3 minutes).
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Stir in vanilla extract.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  6. Add flour mixture to butter mixture alternately with milk, beginning and ending with flour.
  7. Mix until just combined - don't overmix!
  8. Divide batter evenly between prepared pans.
  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
  10. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then remove to wire racks to cool completely.
  11. Frost with your favorite buttercream frosting if desired.
  12. Slice and serve!
